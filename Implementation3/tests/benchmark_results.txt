Architectural note:
  The 123 ms mean reflects the structural cost of replacing a duplex WebSocket
  channel with two separate HTTP legs (POST to publish + SSE to subscribe).
  Each update incurs a full HTTP request/response before the SSE push is
  dispatched — approximately 2x the round-trip latency of the WebSocket relay.
  This is a known trade-off of Pub-Sub over HTTP: better firewall traversal and
  tooling compatibility at the cost of higher per-message latency.
